Crystals Collector!

Try to match your Crystal Count with the amount the computer chooses!

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching you total score with the random number. You lose if your total score goes over the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game restarts the value of each crystal will change.
